## Health Expenditure & Outcomes Dashboard

### Question
How does health spending impact life expectancy, equity, and health outcomes across countries?

### Dataset
Global health expenditure dataset containing information on country-level spending, life expectancy, and health indicators.

### What I Analyzed
- Health spending trends over time
- Life expectancy comparisons across countries
- Relationship between spending and outcomes
- Identification of outliers and inefficiencies

### Key Insights
- Higher spending does not always guarantee better outcomes
- Some countries achieve better life expectancy with lower spending
- There are clear inequalities in health investment and outcomes
- Efficiency varies widely across regions

### Tools Used
Power BI | Power Query
