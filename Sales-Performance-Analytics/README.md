## Sales Performance Analytics Dashboard

### Question
What factors drive revenue, profit, and sales performance across products and regions?

### Dataset
Explored Sample Sales Data containing multi-country, multi-product transaction records. This dataset mimics real-world commercial analytics datasets used in retail, FMCG, supply chain, and financial modeling.


### Key Insights
- Germany emerged as the top-performing market, contributing the highest revenue, followed closely by the U.S., France, Canada, and Mexico.
- Top-selling products such as Velo, VTT, and Montana drive majority of revenue, indicating strong product-market fit and demand concentration.
- Government and Enterprise segments account for over 80% of total revenue, signaling a predominantly B2B-driven business model.
- Revenue peaked in October, suggesting seasonality or bulk procurement cycles within major segments.
- Discount vs Profit analysis shows that some markets maintain high profitability despite lower discounts, pointing to strong pricing power and efficient cost structures.
