## Netflix Content Analytics Dashboard

### Question
What trends exist in Netflix movies and TV shows across genres, countries, and release years?

### Dataset
Netflix dataset containing information about titles, genres, countries, release years, ratings, and content type (Movies or TV Shows).

### What I Analyzed
I analyzed over 8,000 titles revealing how the platform evolved from hollywood dominance to global storeytelling diversity.

### Key Insights
- Movies dominate the catalog at 70%, while TV shows account for 30%.
- The United States leads globally in content production, followed by India and the U.K.
- Netflix saw a huge surge in new titles after 2015, driven by global expansion and original content.
- Drama, Documentaries, and Comedies are the platform’s top genres.
- TV shows have faster growth than movies despite lower total volume.
- Netflix’s library reflects a truly global entertainment ecosystem, shaped by diverse creators, genres, and viewing trends.
